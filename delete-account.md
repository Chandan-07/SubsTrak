# Delete Account & Data

Last updated: March 2026

If you would like to delete your account and any associated data from the Subscription Tracker app, please follow the steps below.

## How to Delete Your Data

Currently, all subscription data is stored locally on your device. You can delete your data in the following ways:

1. Open the **Subscription Tracker** app.
2. Delete subscriptions from the dashboard.
3. Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all stored data from your device.

## Request Account Deletion

If you signed in using Google and would like your account access removed, you may send a deletion request to the developer.

Email: **[your-email@example.com](mailto:your-email@example.com)**

Please include:

* The email used for sign-in
* A request to delete your account data

We will process deletion requests within 7 days.

## What Data Will Be Deleted

When your request is processed:

• Any account access linked to the app will be removed
• Any associated data (if stored) will be deleted
