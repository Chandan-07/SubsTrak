# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the application **Subscription Tracker** ("the App") handles user information.

## Information We Collect

The app does **not collect, store, or share any personal information on external servers**.

All subscription data entered by users (such as service name, price, and renewal dates) is stored **locally on the user's device only**.

## Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

**Notifications**
Used only to send reminders about upcoming subscription renewals or free trial expirations.

The app does **not access contacts, location, or personal files**.

## Data Storage

All subscription information is stored locally using secure on-device storage. The developer does not have access to this information.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party analytics or advertising services that collect user data.

## Data Sharing

We do **not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties**.

## Children's Privacy

This application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Any updates will be reflected on this page.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer.
